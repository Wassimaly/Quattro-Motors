About Us

Welcome to Quattro Motors

For more than 10 years, we have been helping customers with all their automotive needs!

Quattro Motors has started as a car dealership where we focused on customers' needs to buy an affordable and quality cars. However, after finding out the dire need for a reliable automotive repair shop where customers can trust. We started to shift our focus solely on repairing cars with only the highest quality (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts.

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our Value

Our mission is old fashioned service with a twist of sophistication. Our state of the art scanners and OEM parts will put you back on track with a piece of mind

Help customers with all their needs with a competitive price and the highest quality product.

Our value is our customers' satisfaction. We build an honest relationship with our customers and provide reliable deadlines.
